# Vehicle Job Status Workflow Review 

## Workflow Overview

The vehicle job status workflow tracks the progress of a customer vehicle from workshop check-in to final collection.

------------------------------------------------------

## Workflow Stages

### 1. Vehicle Received

* Vehicle arrives at the workshop
* Customer and vehicle details are checked to confirm vehicle is linked to the correct customer
* A job card is created

### 2. Inspection

* Mechanic inspects the vehicle
* Faults/issues are identified
* Repair requirements are documented

### 3. Quote Created

* Estimated repair/service costs are prepared
* Quote is linked to the job card

### 4. Waiting for Customer Approval

* Customer reviews the quote
* Repairs cannot begin until approval is received

### 5. Repair In Progress

* Workshop begins repair/service work
* Job card status is updated accordingly

### 6. Waiting for Parts/ Parts Request

* Required parts are unavailable / requested / ordered
* Repair progress may pause until parts arrive

### 7. Repair Completed

* Repair work is completed
* Final checks for the vehicle may be performed

### 8. Ready for Collection

* Customer is notified that vehicle is ready to collect
* Invoice/payment process may begin

### 9. Collected

* Customer collects vehicle
* Job card is closed
